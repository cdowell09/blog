The week ending Friday, March 7, 2025 was light on major product launches. Two verifiable OpenAI announcements landed in this window.

## 1) NextGenAI consortium launch (March 4, 2025)

OpenAI announced **NextGenAI**, a consortium with 15 research and education institutions, and committed **$50 million** in research grants, compute funding, and API access.

- Source: [Introducing NextGenAI](https://openai.com/index/introducing-nextgenai/)
- Why it matters: This is a large education and research investment, with direct funding and tooling support for universities, hospitals, and public institutions.

## 2) ChatGPT macOS app IDE editing rollout (March 6, 2025)

OpenAI announced that the ChatGPT macOS app could now edit code directly in IDEs for Plus, Pro, and Team users, with broader rollout planned later.

- Source: [OpenAI Developer Community announcement](https://community.openai.com/t/chatgpt-macos-app-can-now-edit-code-directly-in-ides/1137195)
- Why it matters: This added a tighter desktop coding workflow and reduced copy-paste friction between chat and editor.
